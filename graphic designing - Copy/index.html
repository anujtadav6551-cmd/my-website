So, What's This Course Actually About?

A 6-month journey from complete beginner to job-ready graphic designer

At its core, graphic design is the art of creating visual content that communicates a message clearly and beautifully. Every logo you recognise, every poster that catches your eye, every social media ad that stops your scroll — that's graphic design at work. Here at Martian Institute, we'll help you turn your creative ideas into stunning real-world designs using the same tools the pros use: Photoshop, Illustrator, CorelDRAW, and InDesign.

If you're wondering about the best way to learn graphic design, the answer is simple — through practical, hands-on skills you'll use every day, long after you've finished the course. That's why every single month of our curriculum ends with real projects that go straight into your portfolio. No theory exams. No fill-in-the-blank worksheets. Just real designs for real (mock) clients.

By the end of Month 6, you won't just "know" graphic design — you'll have a portfolio of 10+ real projects: a complete brand identity, a magazine spread, a packaging design, social media campaigns, photo manipulations, and a polished logo set. You'll also have a Behance/Dribbble portfolio ready to show employers, a professional CV, and the confidence to walk into any design interview.

Our students have gone on to work at advertising agencies, design studios, print houses, and as freelance designers earning ₹25,000–₹40,000 per month within their first year. Some have even started their own design businesses. Whether you want a full-time job, freelance freedom, or just to level up your creative skills, this course gives you the foundation to get there.

Prerequisites: None. You don't need to know how to draw. You don't need any prior software experience. You just need a willingness to learn, a laptop (or you can use our lab systems), and 6 months of focused effort.

Why are businesses always looking for good graphic designers? Because great design helps them:

  • Build a strong, memorable brand that people instantly recognise.
  • Stand out from competitors with eye-catching logos, brochures, and social media content.
  • Turn casual viewers into loyal customers through powerful visual storytelling.

Where Will Your Skills Take You?

Four industries that are always hiring skilled graphic designers

Once you know your way around Photoshop, Illustrator, and CorelDRAW, the career opportunities are practically endless. Every business — from your local café to multinational brands — needs great visual content. Logos, brochures, Instagram posts, product packaging, magazine ads, YouTube thumbnails... all of it is graphic design work, and all of it is in demand.

Hover (or tap) the cards below to see the four biggest industries where our graduates are working today. Each one has its own style, software focus, and salary range — so you can pick the path that excites you most.

Branding & Advertising

Brand Identity & Ads

Design logos, business cards, letterheads, and full brand identity kits that make companies stand out. You'll learn how to create consistent visual identities and eye-catching ad creatives that leave a lasting impression on customers.

Print & Publishing

Magazines, Brochures & Flyers

Create stunning print layouts for magazines, brochures, catalogues, and posters. You'll master multi-page document design in InDesign, learn about print specifications, colour modes (CMYK vs RGB), and how to prepare files for professional printing.

Social Media & Digital

Digital Content Creation

Design scroll-stopping social media posts, Instagram stories, YouTube thumbnails, and digital ad banners. You'll learn the right dimensions, visual trends, and design hacks that make content go viral and boost engagement.

Packaging & Product

Product Packaging Design

Design eye-catching product packaging, labels, and mockups that jump off the shelf. You'll learn about packaging materials, die-cut templates, 3D mockups in Illustrator, and how to create designs that sell products at first glance.

Web & UI Design

Website & App Layouts

Design beautiful website layouts, app interfaces, and digital experiences. You'll learn UI design principles, wireframing, and how to create pixel-perfect mockups that developers can build.

Photography & Editing

Photo Retouch & Composites

Master the art of photo retouching, skin smoothing, background removal, and creative composites. You'll learn advanced Photoshop techniques used by professional photo editors and ad agencies.

Motion Graphics

Animated Logos & Intros

Create eye-catching motion graphics — animated logos, YouTube intro videos, social media motion ads, and explainer animations. A fast-growing field that pays premium rates for skilled designers.

Signage & Outdoor

Billboards & Hoardings

Design large-format graphics — billboards, hoardings, vehicle wraps, exhibition stalls, and store signage. You'll learn print production at scale and how to design for viewing from 50 meters away.

Our 6‑Month Graphic Design Curriculum

6 modules spread over 6 months — from design fundamentals to a polished portfolio

We’re big believers in learning by doing. Every single month, you’ll design real projects that go straight into your portfolio — by the end of the course, you’ll have 12+ professional pieces to show employers. The first 2 modules (Months 1–2) build your foundation — design principles, colour, typography, and Photoshop. The middle 2 modules (Months 3–4) teach you the core vector and print tools — Illustrator, CorelDRAW, and InDesign. The final 2 modules (Months 5–6) pull it all together into a complete brand identity and a job-ready portfolio.

Just hover (or tap) over any month below to see exactly what we’ll cover, the tools you’ll learn, and the projects you’ll build that month. Tap the + button below Month 3 to reveal Months 4–6.

  • What is graphic design? – Difference between graphic, UI/UX, and web design
  • Elements of design – Line, shape, colour, texture, space, form
  • Principles of design – Balance, contrast, alignment, repetition, proximity
  • Design thinking – Empathise, define, ideate, prototype, test
  • Visual hierarchy – How to guide the viewer’s eye through a design
  • Colour theory – Colour wheel, complementary, analogous, triadic schemes
  • Colour psychology – How colours influence emotions & brand perception
  • RGB vs CMYK – Understanding colour modes for screen and print
  • Typography fundamentals – Font categories, pairing, hierarchy, kerning, leading
  • Choosing fonts for brands – Serif vs sans-serif, readability, Google Fonts
  • Project: Create a mood board, a principles-driven poster, and a colour palette + typography system for a mock brand
  • Photoshop workspace – Tools, panels, menus, layers, shortcuts
  • Working with layers – Layer styles, blending modes, opacity, grouping
  • Selection tools – Marquee, lasso, magic wand, quick selection, pen tool
  • Image editing basics – Cropping, resizing, resolution (DPI/PPI), colour correction
  • Photo manipulation – Retouching, skin smoothing, dodge & burn, clone stamp
  • Text effects – 3D text, metallic text, neon glow, text on path
  • Filters & effects – Blur, sharpen, liquify, lighting effects
  • Masking & compositing – Layer masks, clipping masks, blending multiple images
  • Project: Create a professional social media banner, a poster, and a creative movie poster using photo manipulation techniques
  • Illustrator workspace – Artboard, tools, panels, properties, shortcuts
  • Vector shapes – Rectangles, circles, polygons, stars, custom shapes
  • Pen tool mastery – Bezier curves, anchor points, tracing
  • Working with paths – Pathfinder, shape builder, compound paths
  • Logo design – Sketching concepts, vectorising, scalable logos
  • Brand mark design – Monograms, wordmarks, combination marks, pictorial marks
  • Advanced path editing – Mesh tool, gradient tool, perspective grid
  • Pattern & texture creation – Seamless patterns, brushes, symbols
  • Project: Create a set of flat vector icons, a simple illustration, and a complete professional logo + brand mark for a mock company
  • CorelDRAW workspace – Interface, tools, docker windows, property bar
  • Drawing & shaping – Freehand, bezier, artistic media, shape tools
  • Text & layout – Paragraph text, artistic text, text on path, text wrapping
  • InDesign workspace – Pages, tools, panels, master pages, margins
  • Multi-page layouts – Brochures, catalogues, magazines, newsletters
  • Styles & automation – Paragraph styles, character styles, table of contents
  • Print preparation – Bleed, crop marks, CMYK, PDF export for press
  • Project: Design a professional business card, letterhead, and envelope set in CorelDRAW, plus an 8-page company brochure ready for print in InDesign
  • Brand strategy – Brand positioning, target audience, brand voice
  • Brand identity kit – Logo, colour palette, typography, imagery style
  • Brand guidelines – Creating a professional brand book with usage rules
  • Stationery design – Business cards, envelopes, letterheads, ID cards
  • Print design – Flyers, posters, brochures, magazine & newspaper ads
  • Packaging design – Boxes, labels, pouches, dielines, 3D mockups
  • Social media graphics – Posts, stories, carousel ads, thumbnails, cover art
  • Print vs digital – Resolution, bleed, colour modes, file formats (PDF, EPS, SVG)
  • Project: Build a complete brand identity system with guidelines document, design a product packaging set, and create a month of social media posts for a mock brand
  • Choose a client brief – Real or mock brand (café, fashion label, startup, NGO, etc.)
  • Plan & research – Competitor analysis, mood boards, design direction
  • Design the brand – Logo, colour palette, typography, imagery style
  • Create deliverables – Business card, letterhead, social media kit, packaging mockup
  • Finalise portfolio – Polish projects, create case studies, presentation layouts
  • Build your portfolio – Behance, Dribbble, or PDF portfolio setup
  • Portfolio presentation – Present your work to the class (mock client pitch)
  • Career session – CV & portfolio review, interview tips, job search strategies
