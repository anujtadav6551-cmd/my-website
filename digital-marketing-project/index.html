Introduction

What Is Digital Marketing?

A simple, plain-language overview of what digital marketing is and why every business needs it today.

At its core, digital marketing is about connecting businesses with the right audience online. Here at Martian Institute, we'll help you turn your curiosity into real, result-driving marketing skills. Over 6 months of structured training, you'll run live campaigns, build a real portfolio, and gain the confidence to take on any digital marketing role. If you're wondering about the best way to learn digital marketing, it's through hands-on campaigns you'll run and optimize from day one, long after you've finished the course.

Why is digital marketing one of the fastest-growing careers today? Because every business — from local Vadodara shops to global brands — needs to be found online. Here's what digital marketing helps businesses do:

  • Reach thousands of potential customers through Google search and social media platforms.
  • Build a strong online brand presence that drives trust, engagement, and long-term loyalty.
  • Generate measurable leads and sales with targeted advertising campaigns that deliver real ROI.

Course Curriculum

Your 6-Month Learning Journey

6 months. 6 phases. 24 weeks of structured learning where every phase builds on the last. Click any phase below to see exactly what we'll cover — and the real project you'll build that phase.

  • Week 1: What is digital marketing? Inbound vs outbound, owned vs earned vs paid media, customer journey
  • Week 2: Marketing goals, KPIs, SMART framework, industry overview, tools ecosystem
  • Week 3: How websites work — domains, hosting, DNS, SSL basics, intro to WordPress
  • Week 4: WordPress deep-dive — themes, plugins, Elementor, building high-converting landing pages
  • Project: Build a fully functional WordPress website for a mock Vadodara business
  • Week 5: How search engines work — crawling, indexing, ranking, the Google algorithm
  • Week 6: Keyword research, search intent, on-page SEO, content optimization
  • Week 7: Technical SEO — site speed, Core Web Vitals, mobile-first indexing, structured data
  • Week 8: Off-page SEO, link building, local SEO, Google Business Profile, SEO tools (Ahrefs, SEMrush, Search Console)
  • Project: Complete SEO audit + local SEO campaign for a real Vadodara business
  • Week 9: Google Ads platform — account setup, search campaigns, keyword match types, ad rank
  • Week 10: Ad copywriting, A/B testing, display campaigns, remarketing, YouTube Ads
  • Week 11: Meta Ads Manager — campaign structure, audiences, custom & lookalike targeting
  • Week 12: Ad formats, budgeting, bidding, conversion tracking, campaign optimization
  • Project: Launch live Google + Meta ad campaigns with real budgets and report on results
  • Week 13: Facebook & Instagram algorithms, content pillars, posting frequency
  • Week 14: Reels strategy, hashtag research, community building, engagement tactics
  • Week 15: Content marketing strategy, SEO blog writing, headline formulas
  • Week 16: Copywriting frameworks (AIDA, PAS), video marketing, YouTube SEO
  • Project: 30-day social media content calendar + published SEO blog + video script
  • Week 17: Email marketing fundamentals — list building, segmentation, deliverability
  • Week 18: Writing emails that convert, automation workflows (welcome, abandoned cart, re-engagement)
  • Week 19: Google Analytics 4 — setup, properties, data streams, key metrics
  • Week 20: Custom reports, dashboards, conversion tracking, data-driven decisions
  • Project: Build a 5-sequence email funnel + GA4 dashboard with custom insights
  • Week 21: YouTube Ads, LinkedIn marketing, B2B lead generation, personal branding
  • Week 22: Freelancing essentials — Upwork/Fiverr profiles, pricing, client proposals, portfolio
  • Week 23: Capstone project — full digital marketing strategy for a real client
  • Week 24: Campaign reporting, certification prep, resume building, interview prep, mock interviews
  • Capstone: Plan and execute a complete digital marketing strategy + receive Martian Institute certificate
